Privacy Policy

App Name: Lo Siento - Medical Spanish
Developer: Ryan O'Connor
Last Updated: January 30, 2025
Summary: This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data.

Information Collection and Use

Lo Siento - Medical Spanish is designed with privacy in mind. The app:

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not require user registration or login
  • Does not access your contacts, location, camera, or microphone
  • Does not store any user data on our servers
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not display advertisements

App Functionality

The app provides offline access to:

  • Medical Spanish phrases with English translations
  • Audio pronunciations (stored locally)
  • Interactive learning interface

All content is stored locally on your device and no data is transmitted to external servers.

Audio Playback

The app includes audio files for pronunciation help. These audio files are:

  • Bundled with the app installation
  • Played locally on your device
  • Not recorded, transmitted, or stored externally

Data Storage

Any app preferences or settings are stored locally on your device using standard iOS/Android storage mechanisms. This data:

  • Remains on your device
  • Is not accessible to us or third parties
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

This app is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13, as no personal information is collected from anyone.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Data Requests

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information to:

  • Request access to
  • Modify or correct
  • Delete or export
